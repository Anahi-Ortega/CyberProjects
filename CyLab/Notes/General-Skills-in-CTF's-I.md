## Numbers
Binary - 1 and 0
Quantities - nunbers represent quantities 
10, 1 million, etc
Digits - we write numbers using digits
examples: X, IIII IIII, 10, etc

Base - define the significance of a digits
roman numerals, tally marks, arabic numerals

binary is base 2 and is 0-1 (for a computer this is either on or off)
decimal is base 10 and is 0-9
